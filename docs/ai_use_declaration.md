## AI Use Declaration

### AI models used in the method

| Model                                     | Platform    | Used for                                              |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| `sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2`  | HuggingFace | Abstract embeddings for topic modeling and similarity |
| `Jean-Baptiste/roberta-large-ner-english` | HuggingFace | NER in acknowledgements                               |
| BERTopic with KMeans                      | OSS library | Topic modeling over embeddings                        |

These models are pre-trained and were not fine-tuned. Their outputs were validated through manual inspection.

Generative AI assistants were used only for documentation support, debugging help, and explanations.
